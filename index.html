PRECISION MACHINING NOTES:
• Formulas Verified: sin(θ), cos(θ), tan(θ) = sin(θ)/cos(θ), cot(θ) = cos(θ)/sin(θ), sec(θ) = 1/cos(θ), csc(θ) = 1/sin(θ)
• Precision: 10 decimal places for machining accuracy | Undefined: tan & sec at 90°±n×180°, cot & csc at 0°±n×180°
• Units: D° = Degrees, M' = Minutes, S" = Seconds | 1° = 60', 1' = 60", 1° = 3600"
• Machining Applications: Compound angles, taper calculations, thread pitches, fixture setups
